Privacy Policy

Data Collection

We collect your attendance data, location (for QR verification), and device information to provide accurate time tracking services.

QR Code Data

QR codes contain encrypted supervisor verification tokens. Each scan is timestamped and logged for audit purposes.

Data Security

All data is encrypted using AES-256 encryption. We do not share your personal information with third parties.

Your Rights

You can request a copy of your data or request deletion at any time through the Help & Feedback section.

Terms & Conditions

Usage Agreement

By using TIME Attendance, you agree to scan QR codes only at designated work locations during your assigned shifts.

QR Code Scanning

Each QR code is unique and time-sensitive. Attempting to scan expired or invalid codes will result in an error.

Attendance Accuracy

You are responsible for ensuring accurate clock-in and clock-out times. Manual edits require supervisor approval.

Account Termination

Misuse of the QR scanning system may result in account suspension. Contact HR for appeals.

Help & Feedback

Frequently Asked Questions

How does QR scanning work?

Point your camera at the supervisor's QR code. The app will automatically detect and verify it.

What if QR scan fails?

Ensure good lighting and hold steady. If issues persist, contact your supervisor for manual entry.

Can I edit my attendance?

Yes, but edits require supervisor approval and are logged for audit purposes.

How is overtime calculated?

Overtime applies for afternoon check-out at 6:30 PM or later, and all hours worked on Friday.
